For customers who can’t / won’t make payment with Debit/Cards on your Odoo Marketplace

Introduce Alternative Payment Method on your Marketplace with Cash Payment Option For Odoo Marketplace Customers. For customers who are new to your Marketplace website; Build trust by enabling Cash On delivery (COD) payment on products.

Choose How To Provide COD on your Odoo Marketplace

Set the Applicability of COD with easy Configuration settings.
Configure how & when to provide Cash on delivery to customers.

On Products

  • Enable For All Products.
  • Enable For Selective products.

Geographically Or Area Wise

  • Enable For Selected Countries.
  • Enable in specific States in those countries.
  • Or within selected zip Codes in a state

On Cart Amount

  • Set minimum Cart amount for COD eligibility.
  • Set Maximum Cart amount to availing COD.
  • For Cart total outside the set limits COD becomes disbaled for the customer.

Auto-Manage Commission From Sellers on Cash On Delivery (COD) Orders

The admin’s commission is auto-calculated from the product or order price as set by the admin.
The commission invoice (Pay To seller Invoice) automatically deducted the admin commission as per the settings.

Detailed Feature List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo Marketplace COD Module

Add COD as Payment Option On Marketplace website

  • Provide Cash On delivery (COD) to customers on Odoo Marketplace.
  • Make it easier for sellers to acquire new customers with Odoo COD.

Simple Integration & Configuration

  • Quickly set up the Payment option with single step integration.
  • Configure How to give COD options to customers.

Choose Applicability of COD for Customers

  • Enable COD for geographically and total amount in cart.
  • Enable COD for specific countries, States or Zip Codes.
  • Provide COD on the basis of Total Cart Amount.

Set Thresholds For COD Applicability

  • Enable COD for specific Total cart amount ranges.
  • Any amount below or above the set range automatically becomes ineligible for COD.

Auto-Manage Commission From Seller

  • Every COD order creates a debit record for admin end.
  • Then, a debit invoice is credited for the seller with (COD amount - commission).
  • The Admin commission is auto-deducted in the Pay-To-Seller invoice generated in the backend.

Store transaction records in Odoo

  • The COD orders on Odoo Marketplace and transaction details are stored in the Odoo backend.
